Sheila On 7

1999

Yogyakarta

Sheila on 7 (known as "The Most Beautiful Anugerah I've Ever Had" for the Malaysia & Singapore release version) is Sheila on 7's first studio album. Released in 1999 which was the debut album of Sheila on 7 in the Indonesian music scene. This album itself carries the single hits "Dan", "We", "J.A.P (Make Me Your Boyfriend)", "Pay Attention, Rani!" and "The Most Beautiful Gift I've Ever Had". Sheila on 7 is one of the bands that revived the era of young bands in Indonesia at the start of the new millennium.
